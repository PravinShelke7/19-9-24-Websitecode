Section IX:

Wizard Tools

This section of the User Manual provides instructions for the screens of the Wizard Tools.  The instructions are presented in a sequence similar to the sequence of pages on the Case Manager.  

The input variables for each screen are defined in this section.  Typically, each input variable requires several entries arranged in one column on the input screen.  For example, up to ten materials can be selected on the Material and Structure screen, and these ten selections are positioned in one column.

 

A.  Build-a-Box Wizard

The Build-a-Box Wizard of Sustain1 is examined in the following paragraphs.   

INPUT VARIABLE

DEFINITIONS

 

 

PACKAGE DETAILS

 

 

 

SELECT PRIMARY PACKAGE FORMAT

You choose primary packaging format  - can, folding carton, or bottle

 

 

ENTER PRIMARY PACKAGE DIMENSION

You input dimensions of primary package, depending on which format is chosen, enter, diameter and height or length, width, and depth  

 

 

LAYER DETAILS

 

 

 

LAYER CONFIGURATION

You can enter your layer  configuration (number by number

 

 

NUMBER OF PRIMARY PACKAGES PER LAYER

Displays the number of primary packages per layer

 

 

NUMBER OF LAYERS PER CARTON

You can enter the number of layers per carton

 

 

NUMBER OF PRIMARY PACKAGES PER CARTON

Displays the number of primary packages per carton

 

 

CORRUGATED CARTON DETAILS

 

 

 

CORRUGATE CARTON DIMENSIONS

Displays the dimensions of the corrugated carton.

 

 

CORRUGATE CARTON AREA

B.  Energy Use Wizard

The Energy Use Wizard of Sustain1 is examined in the following paragraphs.   

INPUT VARIABLE

DEFINITIONS

 

 

GEOGRAPHIC REGION

An option to choose from a specific geographic region of interest for the evaluation

 

 

DATE

The date from which the energy value data was calculated

 

 

ENERGY SOURCE (UNITS)

The list of possible energy source mix used to produce electricity

 

 

PRODUCTION MIX - SUGGESTED(%)

Displays the percent of total energy mix for each category of generated energy.

 

 

PRODUCTION MIX - PREFERRED (%)

You can enter your own preferred percent of energy mix for each category

 

 

ENERGY USE RATIO - SUGGESTED (UNITLESS)

Displays energy use ratio that converts to energy for each category of production mix

 

 

ENERGY USE RATIO - PREFERRED

(UNITLESS)

You can enter your own preferred energy use ratio for each category of production mix

 

 

LOSSES AT MANUFACTURING FACILITY - SUGGESTED (%)

Displays the percent of energy loss at the manufacturing facility for each category of production mix

 

 

LOSSES AT MANUFACTURING FACILITY - PREFERRED (%)

You can enter your own percent of energy loss at the manufacturing facility for each category of production mix

 

 

 

TRANSMISSION LOSSES - SUGGESTED (%)

Displays the percent of energy loss through transmission for each category of production mix

 

 

TRANSMISSION LOSSES - PREFERRED (%)

You can enter your own percent of energy loss through transmission for each category of production mix

 

 

 

TOTAL ENERGY USE RATIO (UNITLESS)

Displays the calculated total energy use ratio for each category of production mix and overall total of energy use

 

C.  GHG Emissions Wizard input screen

The GHG Emissions Wizard input screen shows the suggested energy sources.  This wizard allows you to define energy sources and their production mix, along with the GHG from electricity and losses specific to the facility at which the production of the product is being made.

 

INPUT VARIABLE

DEFINITIONS

 

 

GEOGRAPHIC REGION

An option to choose from a specific geographic region of interest for the evaluation

 

 

DATE

The date from which the GHG emissions data was calculated

 

 

ENERGY SOURCE (UNITS)

The list of possible energy source mix used to produce electricity

 

 

PRODUCTION MIX - SUGGESTED(%)

Displays the percent of total GHG emissions mix for each category of generated energy.

 

 

PRODUCTION MIX - PREFERRED (%)

You can enter your own preferred percent of GHG emissions mix for each category

 

 

GHG FROM ELECTRICITY GENERATION - SUGGESTED

(CO2/KWH)

Displays the percent of total GHG emissions from the generation of electricity for each category of generated energy.

 

 

GHG FROM ELECTRICITY GENERATION - PREFERRED

(CO2/KWH)

You can enter your own preferred percent of total GHG emissions from the generation of electricity for each category of generated energy

 

 

LOSSES AT MANUFACTURING FACILITY - SUGGESTED (%)

Displays the percent of total GHG emissions loss at the manufacturing facility for each category of production mix.

 

 

LOSSES AT MANUFACTURING FACILITY - PREFERRED (%)

You can enter your own percent of GHG emissions loss at the manufacturing facility for each category of production mix

 

 

TRANSMISSION LOSSES - SUGGESTED (%)

Displays the percent of total GHG emissions lost in transmission for each category of production mix.

 

 

TRANSMISSION LOSSES - PREFERRED (%)

You can enter your own percent of total GHG emissions lost in transmission for each category of production mix.

 

 

TOTAL GHG -  (CO2/KWH)

Displays the calculated total GHG emissions for each category of production mix and overall total of GHG emissions
